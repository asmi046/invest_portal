• Project name: reconstruction and modernization of a meat-processing plant in Konyshevka village, Kursk region
  • Investor: KURSK AGRO PRODUCT LLC (go to the website)
  • Project implementation dates: 2023-2024.
  • The content of the project: the construction of a new main building and workshops, a sink and a waste treatment system, dormitories.
  • Jobs created: 223
  • Financing, billion rubles: 4,349 billion rubles

Agropromkomplektatsiya Group of Companies, one of the TOP 10 pork producers in Russia, is a major investor investing in the economy of the Kursk region, including in the development of the pig farming sector. Three pig breeding complexes were built and put into operation for 52 thousand heads of one-time maintenance each (with a production capacity of 12 thousand tons of pork in live weight) on the territory of Zheleznogorsk, Konyshevsky and Dmitrievsky districts. The construction of two more complexes with similar production characteristics in Konyshevsky district has been launched.
