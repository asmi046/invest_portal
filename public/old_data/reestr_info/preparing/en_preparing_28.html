• Project name: multidisciplinary regional children's clinical hospital of the 3rd level
  • Investor: The Government of the Kursk region (go to the website)
  • Project implementation dates: 2021-2026.
  • Project content: The new healthcare facility will house an inpatient building with 320 beds, 5 surgical and 9 somatic departments, an anesthesiology and intensive care unit, as well as 4 operating units and a day hospital with 50 beds. The contract for construction work was awarded to Construction Holding Tezis LLC (SHT)
  • Financing, billion rubles: 8245.024 billion rubles

The current status of the project: now a pit has already been developed at the facility, piles have been hammered, walls, grillings and basement columns have been concreted. Also, the floor slabs of the basement of the third building were installed here and temporary roads were equipped. At the moment, the contractor is installing columns of the second building and erecting floor slabs of the 1st and 2nd floors of the third building.
