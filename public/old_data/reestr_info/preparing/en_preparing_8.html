The AgroTerra group of companies is engaged in the cultivation, storage and sale of agricultural crops. We are among the top 20 largest owners of agricultural land in Russia according to BEFL, cultivating more than 200 thousand hectares.

The main crops we grow: soybeans, wheat, sugar beets, sunflowers, rapeseed. In addition, we have built a full production cycle for wheat and soybean seeds.

Direct production is carried out by farms located in the Kursk, Lipetsk, Penza, Ryazan, Tambov and Tula regions. Product safety is ensured by 19 own bases and elevators with a total capacity of more than 500 thousand tons.

Since 2015, the company has had its own Technology Development Center. The Center has more than 600 hectares of experimental fields, agrochemical and seed laboratories, as well as a network of laboratory points at elevators.
