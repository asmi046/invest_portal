Kurskenergo was founded in May 1958 as a regional energy department providing unified management of local energy. During this period, intensive construction of power lines and substations of various voltage classes began, which gave a powerful impetus to the development of industry and agriculture in the region.

Today Kurskenergo is a backbone grid enterprise in the Kursk region. Actively interacting with the leadership of the region, it successfully solves state tasks in the field of electric power industry in its area of ​​responsibility, develops and improves the efficiency of the distribution network complex, provides conditions for technological connection of small and medium-sized businesses, large consumers, pursues a policy of energy and resource conservation, and creates a responsible population's attitude towards energy resources.
