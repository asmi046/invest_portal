• Project name: construction of a fruit storage facility for 5,000 tons in the Belovsky district of the Kursk region on the territory of the Malosoldatsky village council
  • Project content: the project provides for the construction of a fruit storage facility with the installation of refrigeration equipment and a sorting line
  • Investor: Pselskoye LLC (go to website)
  • Project implementation period: 2022 - 2024.
  • Financing, billion rubles: 563 billion rubles
  • Current status of the project: construction of stage 1 has been completed. Construction of phase 2 has begun.

Agroholding "Pselskoye" is one of the largest closed-cycle production farms in the Kursk region, which is engaged in the cultivation of agricultural products, the production of pork and beef meat and its subsequent processing.
