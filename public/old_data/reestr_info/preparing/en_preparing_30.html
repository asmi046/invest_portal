• Project name: urban electric transport upgrade
  • Investor: The Government of the Kursk region (go to the website) and "Movista Regions" (go to the website)
  • Project implementation dates: 2022-2024.
  • Project content: The concession project involves a comprehensive upgrade of the infrastructure and rolling stock of urban electric transport. As part of the initiative, 22 single-section trams will be purchased, 42.6 km of tracks, 8 traction substations and depots will be reconstructed, as well as repair and construction of a contact network. These measures will allow increasing passenger traffic from 7.5 to 8 million passengers per year by 2035. At the same time, the Kurds will be able to save up to 5 minutes of travel time on average, and tax revenues to the regional budget will amount to 451 million rubles for the entire duration of the project.
  • Financing, billion rubles: 12,941 billion rubles
