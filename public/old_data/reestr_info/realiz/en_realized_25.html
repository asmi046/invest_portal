• Project name: Construction of a dairy livestock complex in Zheleznogorsk district
  • Investor: Agropromkomplektatsiya Kursk LLC (go to the website)
  • Project implementation period: 2020-2021.
  • Financing, billion rubles: 4507 million rubles

The construction of a dairy cattle breeding complex in the Zheleznogorsk district, designed for keeping and milking cows for 3,230 cattle with a point for receiving, primary processing of milk (including refrigeration and storage of dairy products), with a site for growing and fattening young cattle of dairy breeds for 3,030 cattle has been implemented.
