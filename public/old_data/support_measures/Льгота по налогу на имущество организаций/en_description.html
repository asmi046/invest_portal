Corporate property tax benefit for participants in regional investment projects

Information about the support measure

Exemption from payment of property tax on organizations of companies participating in regional investment projects in in relation to immovable property related to fixed assets, newly created or acquired during the implementation of a regional investment project worth more than 2 billion rubles, for five tax periods.

Terms of service

Recipient's category

Legal entities are participants in regional investment projects.

Documents for receiving the service

Provision of additional documents to the executive authorities of the Kursk region is not required.

Law of the Kursk region dated 11/26/2003 no. 57-West Kazakhstan Region "On Corporate Property Tax"
