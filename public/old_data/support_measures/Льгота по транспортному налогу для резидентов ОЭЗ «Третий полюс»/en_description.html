Information about the support measure

Exemption from payment of transport tax of organizations — residents of the special economic zone for ten tax periods, starting from the tax period in which the vehicle was registered in accordance with the procedure established by the legislation of the Russian Federation.

Recipient's category

Legal entity — resident of the SEZ

Documents for receiving the service

Provision of additional documents to the executive authorities of the Kursk region is not required.

THE BASIS FOR PROVIDING

The Law of the Kursk region dated 06/02/2020 No. 44-West Kazakhstan Region "On transport tax"
