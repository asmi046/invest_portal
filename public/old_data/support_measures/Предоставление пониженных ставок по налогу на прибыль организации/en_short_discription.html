Information about the benefit

In the Kursk region, as in most regions of Russia, there are reduced income tax rates organizations. This means that instead of the standard rate of 20% (17% to the federal budget and 3% to the regional budget), organizations can pay less.

Reduced rates can be provided under special investment contracts (SPIC), which They are concluded between the investor and the regional authorities. Under such contracts, the investor undertakes to invest a certain amount is invested in the development of production in the region, and in return receives benefits, including reduced income tax rates.
