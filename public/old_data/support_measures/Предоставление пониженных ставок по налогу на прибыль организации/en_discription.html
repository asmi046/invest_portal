Recipient category

Providing income tax benefits to companies participating in regional investment projects

Information about the support measure

Establishment of a reduced corporate income tax rate of 10 percent for companies participating in regional investment projects for three to five tax periods (depending on the volume of capital investments).

Terms of service

30 business days.

Recipient's category

Legal entities are participants in regional investment projects.

Documents for receiving the service

  1. An application in any form for the inclusion of an organization in the register of participants in regional investment projects/making changes to the information, contained in the register that are not related to the termination of the status of a participant in a regional investment project
  2. Copies of the constituent documents with amendments and additions, certified by the applicant. The originals of the documents are also submitted to verify the copies certified by the applicant with the original.
  3. A copy of the document confirming the fact of making an entry on the state registration of the organization in the Unified State Register of Legal Entities, certified by the applicant
  4. A copy of the certificate of registration of the organization with the tax authority, certified by the applicant.
  5. Investment declaration (with the attachment of the investment project) in the form approved by Order of the Federal Tax Service of Russia dated 05.02.2014 No. MMV-7-3/38@ (as amended. Order of the Federal Tax Service of Russia dated 04/17/2017 No. MMV-7-3/315@)
  6. The business plan of the investment project, including the financial model of the project (hard copy and in electronic form), developed taking into account the Methodological recommendations for evaluating the effectiveness of investment projects approved by the Ministry of Economic Development of Russia, the Ministry of Finance of Russia, Gosstroy of Russia on 06/21/1999 VK 477, and recommendations for the development of business plans approved by the order of the Committee for Economics and Development of the Kursk region.
  7. Copies of the balance sheet (OKUD 0710001 form) and the financial results report (OKUD 0710002 form) for the last reporting year (with a mark from the tax authority or with a receipt for admission via telecommunications channels, certified with an electronic digital signature) and for the last reporting date, certified by the organization, as well as a transcript of accounts payable and receivables
  8. The applicant's consent to provide the financial model of the investment project by the Committee for Economics and Development of the Kursk Region to the members of the Commission for evaluating the effectiveness of investment projects (hereinafter referred to as the members of the commission) upon written request with the obligation for the members of the commission to respect the confidentiality of the information received.
  9. Report on form 1-T "Information on the number and wages of employees" for the last reporting period.
  10. Information signed by the applicant on his compliance with the requirements of subparagraphs "b", "c" of paragraph 3 of part 3 of Article 10.1 of the Law of the Kursk region "On Investment activities in the Kursk region".

THE BASIS FOR PROVIDING

The Law of the Kursk region dated 12/14/2010 No. 112-West Kazakhstan Region "On lowering the tax rate of corporate income tax to be credited to the regional budget for certain categories of taxpayers"

Providing income tax benefits to companies participating in special investment contracts

Information about the support measure

Establishment of a reduced corporate income tax rate in the amount of: 0% — for five tax periods, starting from the tax period in which, according to tax accounting data, the first profit from the sale of goods was received, produced as part of the implementation of an investment project for which a special investment contract has been concluded; 5% — from the sixth to the tenth tax period inclusive, 13.5% — the eleventh tax period.

Terms of service

—

Recipient's category

Legal entities participating in special investment contracts

Documents for receiving the service

Provision of additional documents to the executive authorities of the Kursk region is not required.

THE BASIS FOR PROVIDING

The Law of the Kursk region dated 12/14/2010 No. 112-West Kazakhstan Region "On lowering the tax rate of corporate income tax to be credited to the regional budget for certain categories of taxpayers"
