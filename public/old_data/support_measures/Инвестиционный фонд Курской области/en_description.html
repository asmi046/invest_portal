The main areas of the foundation's work include:

  1. Attracting investments in various sectors of the economy, such as agriculture, industry, tourism and other.
  2. Creating a favorable investment climate in the region.
  3. Support for small and medium-sized businesses.
  4. Infrastructure development and creation of new jobs.
  5. Holding events to promote the Kursk region as an attractive place for investment.

The Fund also actively cooperates with other organizations and institutions, such as banks, government agencies, scientific and educational institutions to ensure effective interaction and maximum support for investors.
