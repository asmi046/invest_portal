Information about the benefit

Investment tax deduction is a measure of government support provided to investors within the framework of the tax legislation. In the Kursk region, as in most regions of Russia, there is a system of investment tax deduction.

The investment tax deduction allows investors to reduce the amount of income tax paid to the budget by the amount of investments in fixed assets. This means that part of the cost of acquisition, modernization or reconstruction fixed assets can be deducted from the amount of income tax.
