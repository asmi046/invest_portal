Information about the benefit

In the Kursk region, various support measures are provided for investors, including the provision of subsidies for repayment of interest on loans. Subsidies are provided as part of the investment support program, which is aimed at stimulating the development of the region's economy and attracting investment.

Subsidies are provided to reimburse part of the cost of paying interest on loans received in Russian credit institutions for the implementation of investment projects. The amount of the subsidy depends on the amount of investment and may to reach up to 2/3 of the refinancing rate of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation.
