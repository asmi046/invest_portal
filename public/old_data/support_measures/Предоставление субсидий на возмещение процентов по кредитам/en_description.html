Recipient's category

Subsidies for reimbursement of part of the cost of paying interest on a loan received from a credit institution for the implementation of an investment project

Information about the support measure

Reimbursement of part of the cost of paying interest on a loan received from a credit institution for the implementation of an investment project.

Terms of service

47 working days.

Recipient's category

Legal entities

Documents for receiving the service

  1. Application for participation in the competition, filled out in accordance with the form established by the competition documentation;
  2. Copies of the constituent documents with amendments and additions, certified by the applicant;
  3. A business plan (on paper and electronic media) for the implementation of an investment project;
  4. For individuals, in case of participation in the project with their own funds in any form, a declaration of income for previous years three years (with a mark from the tax authority) and information from banks confirming the existence of savings;
  5. For legal entities — copies of balance sheets (form according to OKUD 0710001) and reports on financial results (form according to OKUD 0710002) for the last reporting year and on the last reporting date, certified by a legal entity with a mark of the tax authority (in two copies), as well as a transcript of accounts payable and receivables to the balance sheets;
  6. Certified by the applicant a copy of the decision of the applicant's authorized management body on the appointment of the general director and a copy of the order on the appointment of the chief accountant;
  7. The register of contracts certified by the applicant for the implementation of construction and installation works and the supply of equipment within the framework of the investment project, indicating the number and date of the contract, the name of the supplier (contractor), the subject and amount of the contract (in the absence of concluded contracts — copies of letters from suppliers (contractors) confirming the readiness of suppliers to sell the necessary equipment (construction work by contractors), indicating the cost and conditions of sale (construction work));
  8. Certificate of the tax authority on open accounts;
  9. When receiving a loan in Russian rubles:
    1. a copy of the loan agreement certified by the bank with a schedule for repayment of the loan and payment of interest on it;
    2. bank-certified copies of statements from the loan account confirming receipt of the loan;
    3. copies of payment orders certified by the investor with the bank's mark on the payment of the amounts of the principal debt on the loan and interest on the loan in accordance with the schedule;
    4. an extract from account 01 "Fixed assets" certified by the investor as of the first day of the month of submission of documents for participation in the competitive selection in respect of fixed assets created or acquired during the implementation of the investment project, with the attachment of background information on the date of acceptance for accounting and the useful life of the above-mentioned fixed assets;
  10. When obtaining a loan in foreign currency for the purchase of imported equipment (all documents are provided in Russian):
    1. a copy of the loan agreement certified by the bank with a schedule for repayment of the loan and payment of interest on it;
    2. certified by the bank copies of statements from the loan account confirming receipt of the loan, or copies of payment orders and (or) documents confirming the opening of a letter of credit for payment of equipment, certified by the bank and the investor;
    3. copies of payment orders certified by the investor with the bank's mark on the payment of the amounts of the principal debt on the loan and interest on the loan in accordance with the schedule;
    4. an extract from account 01 "Fixed assets" certified by the investor as of the first day of the month of submission of documents for participation in the competitive selection in respect of fixed assets created or acquired during the implementation of the investment project, with the attachment of background information on the date of acceptance for accounting and the useful life of the above-mentioned fixed assets;

THE BASIS FOR PROVIDING

Kursk Region Law No. 12.08.2004 37-West Kazakhstan Region "On investment activities in the Kursk region"

Subsidies for reimbursement of part of the cost of paying interest on a loan received from a credit institution to pay the lessor the first lease payment for equipment, purchased under lease for the implementation of an investment project

Information about the support measure

Exemption from payment of property tax on organizations of companies participating in regional investment projects in relation to real estate, related to fixed assets, newly created or acquired during the implementation of a regional investment project worth more than 2 billion rubles, for five tax periods.

Terms of service

—

Recipient's category

Legal entities are participants in regional investment projects.

Documents for receiving the service

Provision of additional documents to the executive authorities of the Kursk region is not required.

THE BASIS FOR PROVIDING

Resolution of the Administration of the Kursk Region dated 07/22/2011 No. 324-pa "On approval of the Procedure for granting subsidies from from the regional budget to investors for reimbursement of part of the cost of paying interest on loans attracted from credit institutions for the implementation of investment projects, to lessee investors for reimbursement of part the cost of paying interest on loans attracted to pay lease payments, or to reimburse the lessee for part of the cost of paying lease payments"

Subsidies for reimbursement of part of the cost of paying lease payments made by the lessee in accordance with the schedule, including interest on the loan, received by the lessee from a credit institution for the purchase of equipment leased to the lessee for the implementation of an investment project

Information about the support measure

Reimbursement of part of the cost of paying lease payments made by the lessee in accordance with the schedule, including interest on the loan, received by the lessor from a credit institution for the purchase of equipment leased to the lessee for the implementation of an investment project.

Terms of service

47 working days.

  9. When the lessor receives a loan in foreign currency to pay for imported equipment to a foreign supplier (all documents are submitted in Russian):
    1. a copy of the lessor's loan agreement, certified by the credit institution, with the loan repayment schedule;
    2. bank-certified copies of statements from the loan account confirming receipt of the loan, or copies of payment orders and (or) documents confirming the opening of a letter of credit for payment for equipment, purchased by the lessee for the lessor;
    3. copies of payment orders certified by the lessee with the bank's mark on the payment of lease payments by the lessee in accordance with the schedule;
    4. a copy of the lease agreement for the supply of equipment to the lessor (lease agreement) certified by the lessee with a schedule for repayment of lease payments by the lessee;

    7. a certificate from the lessor stating that the leased equipment is not the subject of a pledge issued to secure the obligations of the lessor;
    8. an extract from account 01 "Fixed assets" certified by the investor as of the first day of the month of submission of documents for participation in the competitive selection in respect of fixed assets, created or acquired during the implementation of the investment project, with the attachment of background information on the date of acceptance for accounting and the useful life of the above-mentioned fixed assets.
