Information about the support measure

Paragraph 9 of Article 395 of the Tax Code provides for exemption from payment of land tax of organizations — residents of the special economic zone (hereinafter — SEZ), with the exception of organizations specified in paragraph 11 of Article 395 of the Tax Code — in respect of land plots located on the territory of the special economic zone for a period of five years from the month of the emergence of ownership rights to each plot of land.

Recipient's category

Legal entity — resident of the SEZ

Documents for receiving the service

Provision of additional documents to the executive authorities of the Kursk region is not required.

THE BASIS FOR PROVIDING

Article of the Tax Code dated 08/01/2022 No. 395 "The Tax Code of the Russian Federation Article 395. Tax benefits
