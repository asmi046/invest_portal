The export strategy of the Kursk region, is an annex to the Strategy for the socio-economic development of the region until 2030, approved by the Law of the Kursk region of December 14, 2020 No. 100- WKO.

In accordance with the requirements of the Regional Export Standard 2.0, the Strategy was approved by the Ministry of Economic Development of the Russian Federation and the Russian Export Center.

The Strategy takes into account the main objectives of the national project “International Cooperation and Export”: achieving the volume of non-resource, non-energy exports and increasing the number of SME exporters.
