All-Russian competition "Exporter of the Year"

The All-Russian competition "Exporter of the Year" is held among large companies, small and medium-sized businesses and individual entrepreneurs who have achieved the greatest success in exporting non-primary non-energy goods, works, services, as well as the results of intellectual activity.

The Exporter of the Year Award was established by the Government of the Russian Federation and is included in the list of measures of the national project "International Cooperation and Export", which was developed in pursuance of the presidential decree "On national goals and strategic objectives of the development of the Russian Federation for the period up to 2024".

The winners and prize-winners of the competition will be determined in two stages. The winners and prize—winners of the first stage are at the level of each federal district will be determined in the period from August to September 2023. Then, from among the companies that took 1st place according to the results of the district stage, the winners and prize-winners of the competition at the federal level will be selected in the period from September to October 2023

The award ceremony for the winners and prize-winners of the federal stage will be held at the International Export Forum "Made in Russia" with the participation of top officials of the Government.

All participants of the Exporter of the Year contest in 2023 will receive favorable conditions for obtaining a VAT refund guarantee for SMEs

Companies that apply for participation in the All-Russian Exporter of the Year competition by July 12, 2023, receive the right to a unique price offer for a VAT refund guarantee for SMEs from the Russian Group export center: symbolic 2023 rubles, regardless of the amount of the guarantee (but not more than 10 million rubles, in accordance with the terms of the product).

Everyone who applies for participation will be awarded a certificate for receiving the service "Warranty. VAT refund for SMEs" from Roseximbank (part of the REC Group, VEB.RF). It can be used not only by the company itself- the participant of the competition, but also any of his "subsidiary" organization or business partner, to whom the participant of the competition is ready to transfer his certificate. This certificate allows you to achieve a 50-fold benefit compared to standard conditions obtaining this guarantee.

To apply for participation in the competition and get a chance to become the best exporter in Russia, follow the link https://www.exportcenter.ru/awards by filling out an electronic questionnaire.

The guarantee, according to which the participant of the competition receives a unique price offer, is undoubtedly a unique opportunity to quickly replenish the working capital of the enterprise. It allows you to refund VAT for exported products within 10-12 days after submitting VAT reports, that is, long before the completion of the tax audit. The next VAT reporting deadline is very soon — July 25.

It is simple to use the gift: when applying for a guarantee, you need to hand over a certificate that The company participating in the competition will receive the "Exporter of the Year" when registering for the competition.

The All-Russian Exporter of the Year Award this year will be awarded in 20 nominations: six main ones (separately for SMEs and separately for large businesses, 12 in total) and five additional nominations (depending on the size of the company, only 8). This year, a new additional nomination for SMEs will appear in the competition: "The best exporter in the field electronic commerce".

The regional stage of the "Exporter of the Year" competition among small and medium-sized enterprises took place in the Kursk region entrepreneurship

Within the framework of the national project "International Cooperation and Export", a regional project is being implemented in the Kursk region the project "Systemic measures for the development of international cooperation and exports".

One of the activities of the project is the holding of the "Exporter of the Year" competition. On April 11, 2023, the Kursk Region summed up the results of the regional stage of the annual Exporter of the Year competition among subjects of small and medium-sized enterprises of the Kursk region according to the results of their activities in 2022.

The main criteria for evaluating the nominees of the competition are:

  • the volume of exports of products per employee;
  • the range of exported products;
  • the share of exports in the total volume of shipped products;
  • export geography;
  • participation in international exhibition and fair events;
  • the number of newly created jobs.

The following small and medium-sized businesses were recognized as winners in 4 nominations:

  • "Exporter of the Year in the field of industry" — JSC TD Quartz (rubber linings for mills);
  • "Exporter of the Year in the field of agro—industrial complex" - Agroproduct LLC (flakes and cereals);
  • "Breakthrough of the Year" — Bel-Pol LLC (down-and-feather mixture);
  • "Discovery of the year" — LLC "Magistral" (beer and soft drinks).

Regional competition "Exporter of the Year" among large business entities of the Kursk region

In order to implement the regional project "Systemic measures for the development of international cooperation and exports" of the national The Committee on Economics and Development of the Kursk Region is holding a regional competition "Exporter of the Year" among the subjects of the project "International Cooperation and Export" on the implementation of the directions of the Regional Export Standard 2.0 large business in the Kursk region.

The competition dates are until May 1, 2022.

Applications for participation in the competition are provided in electronic form to the official mail of the Committee for Economics and Development of the Kursk region infr.econom@rkursk.ru until April 11, 2022.

The objectives of the Competition are:

  • identification and encouragement of enterprises producing goods, works and services that have achieved the highest indicators in terms of exports in your industry;
  • improving the competitiveness of Kursk region products in foreign markets;
  • the increase in the number of exporters of the Kursk region;
  • diversification of export supplies by nomenclature and countries;
  • stimulation and development of export-oriented production in the Kursk region;
  • stimulating the growth of regional exports and increasing the volume of products in its structure with high share of added value;
  • popularization of the successful export experience of the Kursk region.

The competition is held in the following categories:

  • "Exporter of the Year in industry"
  • "Exporter of the year in the field of agro-industrial complex"
  • "Exporter of the Year in the service sector"
  • "Exporter of the Year in the field of high technologies"
  • "Breakthrough of the Year"

Information about the results of the competition, the rejection of the application for participation, as well as the awarding of the winners of the competition it will be notified additionally, according to the contact details of the responsible employees of the enterprises indicated in the application.

REC begins accepting applications for participation in the "Exporter of the Year" competition

The Russian Export Center announces the launch of the All-Russian competition "Exporter of the Year" in 2021 and begins accepting applications.

Participation in the competition is free of charge. Large companies, small and medium-sized businesses and individual entrepreneurs can apply. Every year the number of participants in the "Exporter of the Year" competition is growing rapidly: if In 2018, about 50 exporters took part in the competition, then in 2020 there were already more than 1,700 of them.

The winners of the competition will be determined in two stages. The winners of the first stage – at the level of each federal districts – we will announce from August to September (we will inform you about the dates of the ceremonies additionally). Then from The winners of the competition at the federal level will be selected from among the companies that took 1st place according to the results of the district stage .

The award ceremony for the winners of the federal stage will be held in November at the International Export Forum "Made in Russia" with the participation of top officials of the state.
