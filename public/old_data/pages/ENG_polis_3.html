In order to attract new residents to the industrial sector of the economy, in accordance with the decree of the Government of the Russian Federation dated February 26, 2022 No. 244, a special economic zone of the industrial and production type "Third Pole" was created in the territory of the Kursk region.

The special economic zone of the industrial and production type "Third Pole" is located on the territory of two municipalities – Zheleznogorsk and Zheleznogorsk district of the Kursk region.

The main purpose of the creation of the Third Pole SEZ is the development of manufacturing sectors of the economy through the creation of modern industrial and production complexes on the territory of the SEZ capable of producing high–tech products of deep industrial processing in order to meet the needs of the Russian economy and stimulate exports.

The goals of the creation of the SEZ "Third Pole" correspond to the strategic development guidelines of both the Russian Federation and the Kursk region and, accordingly, the investment priorities of the country and the region.

Agreements of intent signed with three anchor residents: Mikhailovsky GBZH LLC, Advanced Engineering Technologies LLC, Zheleznogorsk RTI Plant LLC

The management company of the SEZ is JSC Kursk Region Development Corporation in accordance with the Agreement on the Management of the special economic zone of the industrial and production type "Third Pole", established in the territories of the municipalities "Zheleznogorsk city" and "Zheleznogorsk district" of the Kursk region dated 04/28/2022 No.C-107-SG/D14.

Advantages of the SEZ "Third Pole"

  • Benefits and preferences - Preferential tax treatment for SEZ residents
  • Support for residents - Comprehensive support for residents in the "one" window mode
  • State guarantees - Guarantees for SEZ residents against changes in the legislation of the Russian Federation on taxes and fees that worsen the situation of taxpayers
  • Availability of infrastructure - Existing infrastructure facilities located on the territory of the SEZ make it possible to serve the production needs of potential residents of the SEZ.
  • Advantageous geographical location - access to the main sales markets and transport arteries of the country
