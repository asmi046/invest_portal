This section presents various land plots and industrial sites available for investment. Each object is accompanied by detailed information, including its location, area, infrastructure, accessibility of communications and other important characteristics.

Investors can find here both ready-to-use land plots and sites that require additional investment in infrastructure.

The “Land Plots and Industrial Sites” section on the Investment Portal of the Kursk Region provides a convenient tool for searching and selecting a suitable investment property. Investors can easily compare different options, evaluate their potential and make an informed decision.

The investment portal of the Kursk region strives to provide the most current and reliable information to help investors make informed decisions and contribute to the development of the region's economy.
