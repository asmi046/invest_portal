Controversial situations, problems with business development, interaction with authorities – these and many other issues will be solved by the Government of the Kursk region.

An entrepreneur or investor can leave an appeal in an electronic service or get expert advice.

A business can send a request, appeal, complaint or suggestion through 9 channels:

  • Hotline Center "My Business" 8 (800) 201-33-77 (call only from mobile) or cpp46@mail.ru;
  • Investment portal;
  • Business meetings with the regional authorities;
  • The Portal Works Together (https://vmeste46.ru );
  • The Governor's mail (glava@rkursk.ru );
  • The Governor's social media (vkontakte) and The Government of the Kursk region;
  • The reception of the Government of the Kursk region (in electronic format);
  • Commissioner for the Protection of the Rights of Entrepreneurs (Internet reception and social networks);
  • Communication channels of the Ministry of Economic Development of the Kursk region (social networks and website), as well as below in the form.

As a result, the entrepreneur receives:

  1. Operator's prompt response
    — A simple consultation. The decision period is 1 day.
    — A difficult consultation. The decision period is 2 days. It is provided by specialists from relevant ministries and municipalities.
  2. The solution of a typical task that requires the involvement of specialized specialists:
    — The solution period is up to 5 days, depending on the type of tasks. It is provided by specialists from relevant ministries and municipalities.
  3. A comprehensive solution to a difficult situation that requires more time and the involvement of several departments:
    — The solution of the problem is controlled by specialists from relevant ministries and departments together with The Center is My Business and Kursk Region Development Corporation.

All business requests will be reviewed by relevant specialists, and entrepreneurs will receive an answer or solution to each request.
