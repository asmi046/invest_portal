This section presents the socio-economic potential of the Kursk region. Statistical data, analytical reports and reviews allow us to better assess the current state and prospects for the development of various sectors of the economy of the Kursk region.

Analytics includes information on such key indicators as investment volume, unemployment rate, industrial production dynamics, agricultural indicators, construction and others.

In addition, in the “Analytics of the Kursk Region” section you can find information about the socio-demographic situation in the region, data on the labor market, education and healthcare.

All this data will help investors make informed decisions about investing their funds in the Kursk region, as well as assess the potential and opportunities for business development in the region.

The investment portal of the Kursk region strives to provide the most current and reliable information to help investors make informed decisions and contribute to the development of the region's economy.
