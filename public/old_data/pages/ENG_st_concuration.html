The development of competition is an important and necessary step towards the formation of a healthy economy, contributing to lower prices and improve the quality of products and services due to the competitiveness of market participants.

In order to implement a systematic policy to promote competition in the subjects of the Russian Federation, Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation No. 768-r dated April 17, 2019 (hereinafter referred to as Decree No. 768-r) approved the standard for the development of competition in the subjects of the Russian Federation.

This concept refers to the economic policy of the state aimed at creating conditions for the development of competition in the markets of goods and services. The competition development standard includes a number of measures and principles that help ensure equal conditions for all market participants, stimulate innovation and improve the quality of goods and services. It also includes measures to prevent monopolization of markets and protect consumer rights.
