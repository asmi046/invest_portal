Bureau of JSC “REC” abroad

One of the priority areas of activity of REC JSC is the formation of an ecosystem of state support for exports, favorable for the expansion of export supplies, not only in the Russian Federation, but also abroad.

Trade missions of the Russian Federation in foreign countries of the Ministry of Industry and Trade of the Russian Federation

Currently, trade missions are harmoniously integrated into the system of the Ministry of Industry and Trade of Russia, which oversees key areas of industrial development in Russia, as well as issues of development of domestic and foreign trade. Direct interaction with the system of trade missions allows us to pursue a unified policy of promotion in foreign markets, including issues of market access, identifying and eliminating barriers, and promoting industrial cooperation with host states.
