Gorshechensky district occupies the eastern regional part of the Kursk region. From the north and west, the district borders with the Kastorensky, Sovetsky, Timsky and Manturovsky districts of its region, from the south — the Staro-Oskolsky district of the Belgorod region and from the east — the Nizhnedevitsky district of the Voronezh region. The territory of the district is 1.4 thousand square meters (4.7% of the territory of the Kursk region).
