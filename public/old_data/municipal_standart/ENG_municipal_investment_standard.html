This section is a valuable resource for investors who are interested in obtaining information about the state of the investment climate in the municipalities of the Kursk region.

This section provides information about the municipal investment standard, which is a set of rules and procedures aimed at creating a favorable investment climate in the Kursk region.

The section "Municipal Investment Standard in the Kursk Region" provides investors with information about how the standard works, what benefits it provides and how it contributes to business development in the region.
